✦ FAQ

Common questions

Is TMPBOX really free? +
Yes, completely free. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no credit card required. Ever.
Can I extend the inbox lifetime? +
Not currently. Each inbox lasts exactly 10 minutes. After that, it's gone permanently. Just generate a new one.
Can I send emails from TMPBOX? +
No. TMPBOX is receive-only. It's designed purely for receiving verification emails and newsletters.
Are my emails private? +
Each inbox is secured with a unique access token. Only you can access your inbox during its 10-minute lifetime.
What happens to emails after expiry? +
All emails and inbox data are permanently deleted from our servers after the 10-minute window closes.
